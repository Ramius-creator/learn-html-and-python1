Headers and Footers

html

(header)
(h1) Welcome! (/h1)
(a href="example1.html") Home (/a)
(a href="example2.html") About (/a)
(a href="example3.html") Contact (/a)
(/header)

(footer)
(p) something, maybe copyright stuff (/p)
(a href="contact.html") Contact me (/a)
(/footer)

Notes:

- (header) is used for content at the top of a page, such as a title, logo, or navigation links.
- You can place links, headings, images, and other elements inside a header.
- (footer) is used for content at the bottom of a page, such as copyright information, contact links, or credits.
- By default, links will appear next to each other horizontally unless CSS changes their layout.
- A footer is not automatically vertical; its layout depends on the elements used and any CSS styling applied.

Welcome!

Home About Contact
